Front Interview App - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

Welcome to Front Interview App! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

Front Interview App is a privacy-focused application. We only collect the following necessary information:

  • Learning Progress Data: Your question status, favorite questions, excluded questions, daily review records, and other learning-related data
  • App Settings: Your personalized settings such as notification preferences, topic scope selections, and export settings
  • Device Information: Anonymous device information used for crash analysis and performance improvement

1.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location information
  • No browsing history
  • No information about other apps on your device
  • No access to your contacts or photos

2. Information Use

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Save your learning progress and provide a personalized learning experience
  • App Improvement: Use anonymous statistics to understand feature usage and optimize user experience
  • Notification Service: Send daily question reminders based on your settings
  • Troubleshooting: Diagnose and fix application issues

3. Information Storage

3.1 Local Storage

All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any server. This includes:

  • Learning progress
  • Favorite questions list
  • Excluded questions list
  • App settings and preferences

3.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your learning data will be synchronized across your devices through Apple's iCloud service. This data is protected by Apple's privacy policy, and we cannot access your iCloud data.

3.3 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in storage
  • App communications use secure protocols
  • Regular security reviews are conducted

4. Information Sharing

We promise:

  • Not to sell any of your personal information
  • Not to rent any of your personal information
  • Not to share your learning data with third parties
  • Not to use your data for marketing or advertising purposes

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple Services

This app uses the following Apple services:

  • App Store In-App Purchases: For processing subscription payments
  • StoreKit: For managing subscription status
  • Notification Service: For sending learning reminders

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

5.2 Analytics Services

We may use anonymous analytics tools to improve the app:

  • Only anonymous usage statistics are collected
  • No personally identifiable information is included
  • You can disable analytics in your device's Privacy settings

6. Children's Privacy

Front Interview App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

7.1 Right of Access

You can view all your learning data and settings in the app at any time.

7.2 Right to Delete

You can:

  • Clear learning progress within the app
  • Cancel subscriptions
  • Delete the app to remove all local data

7.3 Right to Export

You can export your learning data as images for backup purposes.

7.4 Right to Control

You have full control over:

  • Notification permissions
  • iCloud sync toggle
  • Data sharing permissions

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Data remains on your device as long as you continue using the app
  • App Deletion: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data
  • Subscription Information: Subscription records are managed by Apple and retained according to Apple's policies

9. International Data Transfers

Since the app's data is primarily stored locally on users' devices, no cross-border data transfer is involved. If iCloud sync is used, data transfer is handled by Apple and follows Apple's data transfer policies.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When updated:

  • We will notify you within the app
  • The update date will be shown at the top of the policy
  • Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy

Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or other prominent means.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or complaints about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • App Store Reviews: Leave a message on the App Store
  • In-App Feedback: Use the "Contact Us" feature on the app's settings page

We will respond to your feedback as soon as possible.

12. Consent

By using Front Interview App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms of this Privacy Policy.
