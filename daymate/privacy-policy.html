Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Daymate is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our countdown event tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

Daymate is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Event Data: The countdown events you create (event name, date, emoji/symbol, and reminder settings)
  • iCloud Sync Settings: Your preference for enabling or disabling iCloud synchronization
  • Theme Preferences: Your selected app theme (light or dark mode)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data from third-party services

2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored in Daymate is used solely to:

  • Display your countdown events in the app and widget
  • Send local notifications for event reminders (if enabled by you)
  • Synchronize your data across your devices via iCloud (if you enable iCloud sync)
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync.

iCloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization:

  • Your event data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud account
  • Data is synchronized across your devices signed in with the same Apple ID
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data - it is controlled entirely by you and Apple
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties. Your countdown events and preferences remain completely private to you.

5. Third-Party Services

Daymate does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only Apple service used is iCloud (optional), which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Notifications

If you enable reminder notifications for your events:

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings or within the app

7. Widget Data

The Daymate widget displays your upcoming events on your home screen. This data is:

  • Stored locally in an App Group shared between the app and widget
  • Not transmitted over the internet
  • Accessible only to the Daymate app and widget

8. Children's Privacy

Daymate does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect personal information.

9. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Events: Swipe left on any event to delete it
  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • iCloud Data: If iCloud sync is enabled, disable it in settings before uninstalling to remove data from iCloud, or manage iCloud data through iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by Daymate (visible in the app)
  • Modify or delete your data at any time
  • Disable iCloud synchronization
  • Export your data (by viewing it in the app)
  • Uninstall the app and delete all associated data

12. International Users

Daymate can be used worldwide. All data processing occurs locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not transfer data internationally or store it on our servers.

13. Compliance

Daymate is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - European Union
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - United States
  • Apple App Store Guidelines
