Our Philosophy

Tiky is designed with one goal: help you stay organized without the complexity. We believe productivity tools should be intuitive, beautiful, and stay out of your way. Whether you're planning a trip, managing daily tasks, or organizing a project, Tiky makes it effortless.

Getting Started

Create Templates

Start by creating templates for your recurring tasks. Go to the Templates page, tap +, and add your tasks. Templates are reusable blueprints for your checklists.

Create Checklists from Templates

When you're ready to work, create a checklist from any template. Each checklist is an instance you can check off and complete independently.

Track Progress

Visual progress indicators show you how much you've completed at a glance. Check off tasks and watch your progress grow.

Focus Mode

Need to concentrate? Focus Mode lets you tackle one task at a time without distractions.
