Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2025

Overview

MyCheck is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our checklist application.

Data Collection

MyCheck does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data, including:

  • Checklist templates
  • Checklists and tasks
  • User preferences

is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and remains entirely under your control.

Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is not synchronized with any cloud services or external servers.

Data Sharing

MyCheck does not share any data with third parties. The app operates entirely offline, and no information leaves your device.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Permissions

MyCheck only requests permissions necessary for its core functionality. The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, or any other sensitive data.

Children's Privacy

MyCheck does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
